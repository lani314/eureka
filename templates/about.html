Why Eureka?

Eureka was inspired by a basic concept: we try too hard to imagine.

But, when do we NOT try? What happens then? It turns out some of our best discoveries come unexpectedly, through random events and unexpected associations

Think about it: The story of Archimedes and his bathtub discovery. The cut-up method of William Burroughs. We are inspired by the random, surreal and unexpected

With this in mind, Eureka was created to let you begin projects -- and then have a magical dash of randomness help you figure out the rest.

But how random is TOO random? Sometimes, random things doen't help us make interesting connections. So, Eureka allows for a mixed "synonym" option to complement the randomness. Once you're creating ideas, just click on the "mix" button to see!

Happy creating!
